How to play AI Ping Pong : Moving The Red Board : You will use your right wrist to move the red board up and down. By moving your wrist up the board will move up and if you move it down it will go down. The Goal : The Goal of the game is to get more point than your opponent. You get points by using the red board to block the bouncing ball from getting into your side.
